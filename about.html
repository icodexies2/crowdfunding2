About Us

Welcome to the Society of Active Volunteers and Environmentalists (SAVE), a dynamic and passionate organization rooted in the heart of City College of Calamba. Founded on the principles of service, community engagement, and environmental stewardship, SAVE is dedicated to making a tangible difference in our society. Our mission is to empower students and community members alike to take action towards creating a better, more sustainable world.

KNOW MORE ABOUT US

We are proud to announce that through our dedicated efforts and the support of our community, we have successfully planted 100 trees, recycled 1,000 materials, and cleaned up 20 areas in our local neighborhoods. These achievements are a testament to our commitment to environmental stewardship and community improvement.
